Meet Shiny

Shiny empowers Creators and their fans by providing them with multiple interaction and monetization possibilities on a multifunctional, blockchain-based platform. Starting with the D-A-CH region, we plan on expanding internationally in 2023.

The ecosystem development

  • Community voting and polls

Version 2

Version 2: The full potential unfolded

In Version 2, Creators will be able to introduce and sell their own blockchain-based digital tokens to the ecosystem and sell them on the Shiny exchange to their fans.

Shiny will move towards a permissioned sidechain of the Ethereum 2.0 blockchain, allowing for better scalability, speed and efficiency in all transactions.

The revolution in content creation and monetization

We are planning on becoming the go-to platform where Creators and fans will connect and interaction with each other without the need for intermediary parties.

Multiple interaction possibilities

Multiple interaction possibilities From social media, streaming to content sharing and a marketplace - Shiny offers a multitude of options for interaction.

High security

As a blockchain-based digital platform, the privacy and data protection on Shiny up to the highest standards of technology.

Incentive mechanisms

Creators will have the possibility to create their custom reward structures to increase the engagement of their fans.

Creator tokens

Every Creator will be able to launch their own tokens in Shiny: Version 2. These tokens will be tradable on the Shiny exchange.
